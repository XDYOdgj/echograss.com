Osh Kosh Girls' Ski Jacket & Snow Bib Set

Stay stylish and cozy on the slopes with our Osh Kosh Girls' ski jacket and snow bib set. Designed for both function and fashion, this essential outdoor gear is built to keep you warm in the cold and comfortable during every adventure.

Colorblocked Design

The colorblocked design of this jacket ensures a bold, eye-catching look without sacrificing practicality. It's perfect for making a statement while staying functional during your winter activities.

Side Seam Pockets

Side seam pockets provide secure storage and added warmth. These pockets are not only functional but also help keep your gear organized on the go, making your ski or snowboarding experience more efficient.

Ankle Gaitors

Adjustable ankle gaitors ensure extra protection from the cold and snow. They help keep your legs warm and dry, so you can focus on enjoying your time on the mountain or trail without discomfort.

Fleece-Lined Jacket

The jacket is lined with soft, insulating fleece to keep you warm in the harshest winter conditions. Its snug fit and breathable fabric make it ideal for all-day use, keeping you both cozy and comfortable.

Poly-Lined Snow Bib

The snow bib features a poly-lined interior to wick away moisture and maintain warmth. Designed to fit seamlessly with the jacket, it's the perfect addition for keeping your lower body dry and protected during snow activities.

Perfect for Outdoor Enthusiasts

This bundled set is ideal for any girl who loves the outdoors and adventure. Whether she's hitting the slopes or gliding through the snow, the Osh Kosh Girls' ski jacket and snow bib offer a reliable and fashionable solution to keep her warm and stylish all season long.
