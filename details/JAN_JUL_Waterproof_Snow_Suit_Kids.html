JAN & JUL Waterproof Snow Suit for Kids

Stay warm, dry, and safe with this versatile snow suit designed for cold weather adventures.

Stay Cozy and Protected

Perfect for snowy days, the JAN & JUL fleece-lined snow suit keeps your child warm and dry. Whether they're sledding, skiing, or heading out for a winter camp, this reliable piece ensures comfort and protection all day long.

Durable and Breathable

Equipped with a fully waterproof inner membrane and taped seams, this snow suit creates a weatherproof barrier against snow and rain. The breathable fabric ensures your child stays warm without overheating, while the adjustable waist with inner toggles offers a snug, personalized fit.

Elastic Bands for Security

The elastic wrist and ankle bands lock in warmth and prevent snow or rain from seeping in, making it ideal for active play in the cold.

Extra Safety Features

Perfect Fit for Every Child

This toddler snow suit features an adjustable design that ensures a secure and comfortable fit, no matter your child's size or activity level.

Why Choose This Snow Suit?

When the weather turns cold, you need gear that keeps your child safe and comfortable.
