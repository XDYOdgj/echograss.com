AOBUTE Rash Guard Shirts for Kids 3-14

Stay protected, stay stylish with our UPF 50+ rash guard shirts. Designed for boys aged 3 to 14, these shirts are built to keep your little ones safe from harmful UV rays while letting them move freely and play all day long.

Key Features

  • UPF 50+ Protection: Our rash guard shirts block 98% of UV rays, making them ideal for sunny days on the beach, lake, or boat.
